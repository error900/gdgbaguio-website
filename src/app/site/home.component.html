what we're about

GDG Baguio is a group of diverse developers who are very much interested in learning and experimenting on different and powerful Google Technologies. We are spreading our love and support for Google Technologies by conducting different events, seminars, and codelabs to help fellow developers kickstart their journey in using these Technologies.
